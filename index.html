Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Here's a time line of Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam:

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram of Madras Presidency.
  • 1969 - Kalam was transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
  • 1980 - Was the Project Director of the (SLV) that successfully deployed the satellite Rohini into orbit.
  • 1992 to 1999 - Kalam was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and the Secretary of the DRDO.
  • 1955 - Went to the Madras Institute of Technology to pursue aerospace engineering.
  • 2002 - Abdul Kalam was elected the 11th President of India.
  • 2015 - Abdul Kalam died of a cardiac arrest while giving a lecture at the IIM Shillong.
